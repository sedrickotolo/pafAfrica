His academic journey includes a Master of Biostatistics in progress at Makerere University, concurrent with a Master of Monitoring and Evaluation at Uganda Technology and Management University. Mr. Nixson holds a postgraduate diploma in Monitoring and Evaluation from Uganda Management Institute and a bachelorâ€™s degree in economics and Statistics from Kyambogo University, forming the base of his expertise in the field.
